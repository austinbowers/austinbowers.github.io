While interning for International Voyager Inc. (a travel agency that runs top rated booking websites), I overheard the head of the company say they were looking for a new design on one of their sites. In my spare time, I researched a variety of cruise websites that they enjoyed, and tried to create a similar design based off of these sites. I stepped up to the plate to take on the task in my free time from work, and designed a mockup front page for their website for Cruise Director (a site offering discounted rates on luxury cruiselines).

Sunshine Series Logo Design

"The Sunshine Series" hosted by Sahara Moon features other artists around the world and gives them the opportunity to spread their music. Sahara was looking for a fun new logo design that she could put on T-shirts, so I designed her a logo that emphasized sunshine, and added some water to remind us that this event takes place on Long Beach Island, a place full of art, music, inspiration, and excitement.
